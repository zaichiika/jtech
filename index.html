Why Me?

Like other skilled front-end developers, I have experience with HTML5, CSS3, SASS, JQuery, and all other usual techie acronyms (my resume has all the details if you need them). But acronyms can’t tell the whole story!

I work hard to make your job easier! Project managers like not having to worry about my deadlines; I stick to the schedule and communicate with the team often to keep everyone up-to-date. I strive to stay true to their creative vision. Technical managers like not having to worry about the quality of the code they’ll receive, well-commented and ready to integrate.

When deadlines loom and the pressure is one, it’s tempting to take coding shortcuts to just “get it done.” I take pride in always going the extra mile to create well-crafted, standards complaint pages using semantic code, be it HTML5, XHTML, CSS or JavaScript.
